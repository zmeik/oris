🎯 Arena — how it works

▸ click to expand
1
Examine the radiograph — anonymised panoramic dental radiograph (OPG) of a real K08.1 patient (5% all-side cropped, EXIF-stripped, watermarked)
2
Fill the ground-truth row (blue) — click any of the 32 tooth cells to set its layered status (e.g. endo:mo+post+crowned). The layer editor opens with the full 18-status × 5-surface grammar.
3
Compare algorithms — each row below the GT shows what one algorithm predicts. Red border = disagreement with GT.
4
Cast votes — 👍 promising, 💀 hopeless. Aggregated κ across the 31-algorithm population is in the Metrics tab.
